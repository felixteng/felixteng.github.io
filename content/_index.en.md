---
title: "Fei Teng"
---

Fei Teng is a Professor and Deputy Director of the Institute of Energy, Environment and Economy (3E) at Tsinghua University, where he also serves as a doctoral supervisor. He holds a B.E. (Engineering) and an M.S. and Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering, all from Tsinghua University.

He served as a Lead Author of the Fifth and Sixth Assessment Reports of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC). He has published nearly 100 papers in Chinese and international journals including *Science* and *Nature Climate Change*, cited over 2,000 times, and has received two provincial/ministerial Second Prizes as a principal contributor. From 2007 to 2017 he took part in multilateral climate change negotiations as a member of China's climate negotiation delegation.

His main research areas are integrated assessment modeling, energy systems analysis, and global and domestic climate policy and governance.
