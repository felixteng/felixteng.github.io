My research integrates economic modeling, energy systems analysis, and policy assessment to inform the transition toward carbon neutrality.

## Integrated Assessment Modeling

Development and application of hybrid integrated assessment models (e.g. the IMACLIM-China family) to analyze greenhouse-gas neutrality pathways, the macroeconomic implications of mitigation, and global cost-sharing of climate action.

## Energy Systems Analysis

Bottom-up analysis of China's energy system transition — power, industry, and end-use sectors — including methane mitigation, negative-emission technologies, and the feasibility of deep decarbonization.

## Climate Policy & Governance

Design and evaluation of domestic and international climate policy, equity in burden-sharing, post-net-zero obligations, and China's role in global climate governance.
